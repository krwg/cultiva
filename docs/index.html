Designed for focus

Everything you need.
Nothing you don’t.

Clarity over clutter — the same philosophy Apple brings to interface design, applied to habit building.

📴

Offline-first

IndexedDB storage on your machine. No account, no sync server, no analytics.

🔥

Streaks & grace

Binary or quantity habits with streak tracking and optional grace for missed days.

📅

Calendar

Plan events alongside habits. Holidays, themes, and ambient backgrounds included.

🧩

Plugin ecosystem

Extend Cultiva with sandboxed widgets — weather, radio, pomodoro, and more. Installed from GitHub with sha256 verification.

💾

Your data, your backup

Export JSON, ZIP, or iCal. Auto-backup on desktop. Full reset when you need a fresh start.

No telemetry
