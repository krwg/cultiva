Built for focus.
Not for feeds.

Everything stays on your machine. No accounts, no sync servers, no analytics.

Offline-first

Habits live in IndexedDB on your device. Cultiva works fully without an internet connection.

Streaks and grace

Binary or quantity habits with streak tracking and optional grace days when life gets in the way.

Calendar

Plan events alongside habits. Holidays, themes, and ambient backgrounds included.

Local search

Header search is powered by glyph-s 2.8.0 — the same offline engine as the Glyph family. Habits, plugins, beds, and settings, on-device.

Plugins

Sandboxed widgets from GitHub — weather, radio (33 stations · ICY · live Neo), pomodoro, and more. Every file sha256-verified. Prefer Cultiva 2.0+; 1.7 support is ending soon.

Themes

Rowan, Linden, Evergreen, Ocean, and a dozen more. Light, dark, and custom accent colors.

Your backup

Export JSON, ZIP, or iCal. Auto-backup on desktop. Full reset when you need a fresh start.

Privacy by architecture — not by policy page.

No telemetry

No tracking pixels or crash reporters phoning home. The app never needs the network to function.

Local storage

Habits and settings live in IndexedDB on your device. Nothing is uploaded to a server.
