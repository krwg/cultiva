# Themes and Backgrounds

Cultiva **2.0.0** ships **19 themes** and **14 ambient background presets**, plus an optional **custom photo**. Theme and background apply to **both** the garden and the **calendar** page.

---

## Themes (19)

### Light group
`light`, `blossom`, `frost`, `cedar`, `meadow`, `orchard`, `honeycrisp`, **`linden`**

### Dark group
`dark`, `evergreen`, `ocean`, `sunset`, `pink`, `moon`, `dusk`, `inkwell`, `sequoia`, `cypress`, **`rowan`**

### Auto
Follows system light/dark preference (`prefers-color-scheme`).

**Linden** (1.7 codename) — soft green palette inspired by Apple Human Interface.  
**Rowan** (2.0 codename) — strict graphite black-and-white.  
**Cypress** (1.1 codename) — deep evergreen.

Change under **Settings → Appearance**.

---

## Ambient backgrounds (14)

`aurora`, `rainfall`, `starlight`, `snowfall`, `fireflies`, `petal`, `mist`, `ember`, `breeze`, `cypress-drift`, `dew`, `sunbeam`, **`linden-bloom`**, **`rowan-cluster`**

Plus **custom** — upload a photo stored locally only.

**Rowan Cluster** — canvas «Pulsing Cluster»: trembling zigzag branches, flickering pinnate leaves, pulsing berry probes with radar rings and falling particles on `#0b0b0b` graphite.

**Intensity** slider adjusts opacity. With **Reduce motion** enabled at the OS level, animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.

---

## Accent color

Optional override for buttons and highlights in **Settings → Appearance**. Reset to theme default anytime.

---

## Title bar

On desktop, the native title bar tint follows the active theme (`shell-chrome.js`).
