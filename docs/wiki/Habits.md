# Habits

## Creating a habit

1. Click **+** in the garden header (or use onboarding)
2. Choose a **template** or start blank
3. Set name, type (binary or quantity), and optional target

## Growth stages

| Stage | Typical meaning |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Seed** | Just planted |
| **Sprout** | Early consistency |
| **Sapling** | Building momentum |
| **Tree** | Strong habit |
| **Legacy** | **365+** days of practice |

Stages are **visual milestones** — they reflect long-term consistency, not just today's checkbox.

## Completing habits

- **Binary:** mark done once per day
- **Quantity:** log progress toward a daily target
- Completing updates **one card** in the garden (no full-page flash)

## Streaks

- Streak counts consecutive days (or periods) you meet the habit goal
- **Grace day** (Settings): when enabled, **one missed day per calendar month** does not break the streak

## Deleting & resetting

- Delete a single habit from its card menu — persists after reload (fixed in 1.7)
- **Full reset** in Settings clears habits, settings, and **installed plugins**

## Calendar interaction

Habits and calendar events are separate but share themes. Navigating to the calendar and back no longer restores deleted habits (1.7 fix).

## Plugins that react to habits

**Streak Celebrator** (official plugin) listens to `onHabitComplete` and shows a toast when you finish a habit.
