Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026. Your privacy is fundamental to how we build Cultiva.

1. Information We Collect

Cultiva operates on a privacy-first principle. We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide core functionality:

  • • Account credentials (email, salted SHA-256 password hash)
  • • Habit data, progress, and settings stored locally
  • • Optional profile information (name, avatar, date of birth)

2. How We Store Your Data

All personal data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. We do not transmit your habits, progress, or profile information to external servers. Account authentication uses client-side salted SHA-256 hashing—passwords are never stored or transmitted in plaintext.

3. Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • • Rendering your personal habit garden
  • • Tracking progress and calculating growth stages
  • • Enabling export/import functionality for backups

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any third-party purposes.

4. Your Rights & Controls

You maintain full control over your data at all times:

  • • Export all your data as a JSON file via Settings
  • • Delete individual habits or clear all data locally
  • • Request account deletion (see section below)

5. Third-Party Services

Cultiva is built to minimize external dependencies. We do not integrate:

  • • Analytics or tracking scripts (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • • Advertising networks or remarketing pixels
  • • Social media widgets that transmit user data

The only external resource loaded is GitHub-hosted fonts/icons, which are cached locally after first load.

6. Data Retention

Since all data resides in your browser, retention is controlled by you:

  • • Data persists until you clear browser storage or uninstall the app
  • • Account credentials are stored only if you explicitly create an account
  • • Guest-mode data is isolated per browser profile

7. Children's Privacy

Cultiva is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via the application interface or GitHub repository. Continued use of Cultiva after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach out via:

  • GitHub Discussions
  • We respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 7 business days.

🗑️ Delete Your Account

Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data. This action is irreversible.
