Privacy Policy

We're crafting a clear and honest privacy policy. In the meantime, here's what you should know.

1

Your data stays on your device

Cultiva is local-first. All habits, settings, and personal information are stored in your browser's IndexedDB. We don't have servers to send your data to.

2

Zero telemetry

We don't collect usage statistics, analytics, or crash reports. What you do in Cultiva is your business — and yours alone.

3

You control your data

Export your habits anytime as a JSON file. Import it anywhere. Delete everything with one click. Your data belongs to you.

4

Open source transparency

Every line of code is public on GitHub. You can verify exactly what Cultiva does — no hidden functionality, no surprises.

The full privacy policy is being finalized and will be published here soon.
Follow updates on GitHub or check back later.
