Watch Your Habits Bloom

Every habit starts as a tiny seed. With consistent care and daily attention, it grows through five distinct stages of development — from fragile sprout to mighty legacy tree.

  • 🌱 Seed (0-7 days) — Plant the seed of change
  • 🌿 Sprout (8-30 days) — First signs of growth
  • 🪴 Plant (31-90 days) — Establishing roots
  • 🌳 Tree (91-364 days) — Strong and resilient
  • 🏆 Legacy (365+ days) — Eternal monument to your discipline
