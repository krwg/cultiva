How it works

Every habit follows the same path. Consistency is the only requirement.

From seed to legacy

Each completed day moves your habit forward. Miss a day, and your streak resets — but your progress stays. It's forgiving, but honest.

  • 🌱 Seed — Days 0–6. Plant the intention.
  • 🌿 Sprout — Days 7–20. First signs of consistency.
  • 🪴 Plant — Days 21–49. Taking root.
  • 🌳 Tree — Days 50–364. Strong and established.
  • 🏆 Legacy — Day 365+. A permanent monument.
Take a look

Your Trophy Garden

When a habit reaches 365 days, it earns a permanent place in your Trophy Garden. These are your legendary habits — the ones that changed you.

  • Golden frame — Instantly recognizable.
  • Custom names — Name your legacy habits.
  • Dedicated gallery — Separate from active habits.
  • Full history — Every completion, preserved.

Cultiva Desktop.

The full Cultiva experience, now as a native Windows application. Offline-first, system-integrated, and completely free.

  • Standalone .exe — No browser required. Launch directly from your desktop.
  • Two options — Full installer with Start Menu shortcuts, or portable version for USB drives.
  • System integration — Native window controls, taskbar presence, persistent window state.
  • Same privacy — Zero telemetry. All data stays on your device.
